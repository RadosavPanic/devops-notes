# Key cloud terminology

## Capital Expenditure (CapEx)

CapEx is large upfront investment into infrastructure.

Companies earlier invested into building own infrastructure like servers and data servers.

## Operational Expenditure (OpEx)

OpEx is ongoing cost for renting resources (or services) as needed.

## Pay As You Go

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Trade capital expense (CapEx) for variable expense (OpEx). Pay only for the resources you actually use.

## Economies of Scale

Buying resources in large quantities lowers the cost per unit.

Cloud providers purchase infrastructure in massive volumes.

- **Result**: lower cost per unit
- **Customer benefit**: Lower prices passed on to users

## Avoid Undifferentiated Heavy Lifting

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Spend more time on what makes your business unique.

**Example**: Insurance company focusing on calculating better premiums. Company focuses on fraud detection and customer experience while cloud provider manages infrastructure.
